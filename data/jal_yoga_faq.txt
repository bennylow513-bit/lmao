*Studio  Policy  
 
To
 
help
 
you
 
get
 
the
 
most
 
out
 
of
 
your
 
studio
 
experience,
 
here’s
 
a
 
quick
 
overview
 
of
 
our
 
key
 
policies:
 
1 ⃣  *Class  Booking  Policy*  
•   Class  bookings  can  be  made  via  the  Jal  Yoga  App  up  to  3  days  in  advance,  starting  from  2:00  PM.  
•   Example:  To  book  a  class  on  31  December,  bookings  open  on  28  December  at  2:00  PM.  
2 ⃣  *Class  Cancellation  Policy*  
•   You  may  cancel  your  booking  without  penalty  up  to  2  hours  before  the  class  starts.  
•   Cancellations  made  less  than  2  hours  before  class  or  no-shows  will  be  considered  late  
cancellations.
 
•   After  3  late  cancellations,  booking  access  on  the  Jal  Yoga  App  will  be  suspended  for  7  calendar  
days.
 
3 ⃣  *Suspension  Policy*  
•   For  non-medical  suspensions,  an  extension  fee  of  S$50  per  month  applies  (maximum  suspension  
period:
 
3
 
months).
 
•   An  extension  beyond  your  membership  expiry  will  be  granted  based  on  the  suspension  duration.  
•   Medical  suspensions  require  valid  documentation  from  a  certiﬁed  physician  for  exemption  from  
this
 
policy.
